We’re excited to announce the initial launch of **Fiona**, a helpful AI assistant
available in the Ed-Fi Alliance community **Slack** workspace.

Fiona is designed to help **educators, technologists, and administrators** get
quick answers about Ed-Fi technology, education data standards, APIs,
implementation guidance, and related tools.

<!-- truncate -->

## How to use Fiona

You can interact with Fiona in two ways:

- **Direct message:** Send a DM to the Fiona Slack app.
- **In-channel:** Tag **@Fiona** in any channel to ask a question where others can
  benefit from the answer.

## Tips

:::warning
Fiona is an AI tool built on the Ed-Fi documentation. As with any AI tool, it
can and will make mistakes.

Please validate guidance before acting on it—especially for production
deployments, data governance, or security-related decisions.
:::

1. **Fiona’s sources of truth are limited** to content published on
   **https://www.ed-fi.org** and **https://docs.ed-fi.org**.
2. If something feels off, ask a follow-up question or request a more detailed
   explanation (for example, “What assumptions are you making?” or “What doc page
   is that based on?”).
3. Use the **thumbs up** and **thumbs down** buttons to provide feedback. When
   you do, Fiona will automatically save **your prompt and the response**, which
   helps us improve quality over time.

## Known Issues

1. In the initial release, Fiona may provide footnote-like citations (for
   example, **[1]**) but does not yet include the actual source links. We’ll get
   that fixed soon.
2. The community will have many great questions that simply are not yet
   addressed in the written documentation. We look forward to gathering those
   questions and finding the best ways to address them.
